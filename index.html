About the Temple

Welcome to the Shri Hanuman Temple, a sacred place of worship dedicated to Lord Hanuman, the epitome of devotion, strength, and perseverance. Our temple has been serving the community for over 50 years, offering a serene environment for prayer, meditation, and spiritual growth.
